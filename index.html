About Me

Hello, I’m Nesia. I love photography and videography. I am a graduate from the University of Ottawa with a BA in Communication which cultivated my passion in working with new media. I have been able to use my skills to tell stories for various clients covering fashion, music and health. I would describe myself as someone who is enthusiastic and adventurous. I love photographing my travels and constantly push myself out of my comfort zone. When I don’t have my camera on hand, I enjoy singing, playing the guitar and petting my cat. All at the same time of course.
